##Promise ᕙ(⇀‸↼‶)ᕗ

An object acting as a proxy ([thenable](http://promises-aplus.github.io/promises-spec/))
for a result ([value/reason](http://promises-aplus.github.io/promises-spec/)) that is initially unknown.
